Project Overview

Our flagship facility in Kutch represents the successful implementation of our bio-energy vision. This operational plant demonstrates our capability to deliver commercial-scale solutions that create value for all stakeholders.

Located in Asambia Village, the facility serves as a model for sustainable waste management and renewable energy production, supporting the local agricultural community while contributing to India's clean energy goals.

Environmental Impact

  • Processes agricultural waste that would otherwise be burned
  • Reduces methane emissions from organic waste decomposition
  • Provides clean energy alternative to fossil fuels
  • Creates valuable organic fertilizer reducing chemical dependency

Technical Specifications

CBG Production 3 TPD
Organic Fertilizer 8 TPD
Status Fully Operational
