Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

Q: How can I purchase your organic fertilizer?

Contact us through the form above or email us at info@tirhuta.in with details about your requirements, including quantity needed and location. Our team will provide pricing and availability information.

Q: Do you accept site visits?

Yes, we welcome site visits to our operational facility in Kutch, Gujarat. Please contact us in advance to schedule an appointment. This helps us ensure safety protocols and provide you with a comprehensive tour.

Q: Are you looking for investment partners?

We're always open to discussions with strategic investors who share our vision for sustainable energy. Please reach out through our contact form or email with information about your investment interests.

Q: Can I supply agricultural waste to your facility?

We work with local farmers and agricultural suppliers in regions where we operate. If you have consistent agricultural waste available, please contact us to discuss potential supply arrangements.

Q: Do you have job openings?

As we expand, we periodically have openings for various roles. Please send your resume and area of interest to info@tirhuta.in, and we'll keep your information on file for relevant opportunities.

Q: How can I learn more about bio-gas technology?

Explore our "What We Do" section for detailed information about our CBG production process and technology. For more specific technical inquiries, feel free to contact our team directly.
