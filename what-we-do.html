Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG)

Converting agricultural waste into premium-quality renewable energy

Our Compressed Bio-Gas facilities represent the cutting edge of waste-to-energy technology. We process agricultural residue, organic waste, and biomass through advanced anaerobic digestion, producing high-purity bio-gas that's compressed and directly integrated into India's city gas distribution networks.

Why CBG Matters

India generates millions of tons of agricultural waste annually. Traditionally, much of this waste is burned in fields, contributing to air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. We transform this problem into an opportunity ‚Äì converting waste into clean fuel that can power vehicles, homes, and industries.

Key Benefits

  • Clean, renewable energy equivalent to natural gas
  • Reduces dependence on imported fossil fuels
  • Significantly lowers greenhouse gas emissions
  • Seamless integration with existing gas infrastructure
  • Long-term supply agreements ensure stability and reliability
  • Supports India's energy independence and SATAT initiative
  • Creates rural employment and economic opportunities

Our Production Process

1
Waste Collection & Preparation

We source agricultural residue and organic waste from local farming communities, creating additional income streams for farmers while solving waste management challenges.

2
Anaerobic Digestion

Organic matter is broken down by microorganisms in oxygen-free digesters, producing biogas rich in methane and valuable by-products.

3
Gas Purification & Upgrading

Raw biogas is purified to remove impurities, upgraded to pipeline-quality standards, and compressed to create high-grade CBG.

4
Distribution & Supply

Compressed Bio-Gas is supplied directly into city gas distribution networks under long-term offtake agreements, ensuring consistent revenue and energy supply.
